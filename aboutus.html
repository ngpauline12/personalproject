The Need

We throw out tons of fruit and vegetables each year because it has gone soft, mouldy or is out of date. In the United States, food takes up more space in landfills than anything else. How much food do we waste? About 40 million tons every year. That's like if every person in the United States tossed 650 apples into the garbage.

What causes food waste? Lack of understanding expiration labels, impulsive food purchases, underutilizing leftovers are just top of the line.
Moreover, wasting food has irreversible environmental consequences-generating 11% of the world's greenhouse gas emissions. Happy Fridge is about making reducing food waste easy and fun, inspiriing and empowering everyone to take action against food waste.

Meet Pauline

Pauline Ng is a Boston-based freelance web developer and graphic designer. Living alone, Pauline quickly learned how difficult cooking for one person was, and how easily it generated food waste. With that in mind, happy fridge was born, with the vision of helping individuals reduce food waste to save money and reduuce our carbon foodprint.
